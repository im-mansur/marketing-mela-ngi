About Marketing Mela

Marketing Mela is an annual event that brings together entrepreneurs, businesses, and customers in a vibrant marketplace atmosphere. Our event showcases the best products and services from local and regional vendors.

Founded in 2010, Marketing Mela has grown from a small local gathering to one of the region's most anticipated business events. We provide a platform for businesses to connect with customers and showcase their innovations.
